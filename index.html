Description of the project The goal of the visualization is to classify the author of children book based on gender which has been described in the dataset. The vis tools that I use here is merely for presentation (supporting people who want to explain something rather than to explore and analyze the unknown). The data will only appear as a bunch of scattered number and text that will be complicated to be understood without representing it as a graph or visualization captured in detail.
